# Plant app

The scope of the app is as follows:
-Users upload and remove photos of their plants, attaching information such as a name, species and other potential categories.
-Users can create login credentials and log in and out of the service.
-Users can see the plants other users have uploaded to the service.
-Users are able to connect their plants with other plants of their choice.
-Users can search for other plants by name or another category.
-The app has a profile page for each plant, showing which plants the user has connected with and how many time the profile has been loaded.
-Connections to other plants are kept track of.
-(Potential features to visualise connections to other plants)
